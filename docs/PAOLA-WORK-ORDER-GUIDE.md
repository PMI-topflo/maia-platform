# Maintenance in MAIA — work orders, estimates, recurring services & board approval

A practical guide for the maintenance coordinator. **Legend:** ✅ live now · ⏳ coming soon.

## A. Work orders

- **Creation** ✅ — a resident/board email becomes a **ticket**. If it's maintenance, classify it as a **Work order** and set the **Work order type** (Roof Repair, Fire safety, …).
- **Sidebar order** ✅ — the work-order sidebar leads with **① Association** then **② Vendor**. Set those first; GL and invoice linking follow.
- **Put it in CINC** ✅ — when it shows "Not in CINC yet," click **＋ Create work order in CINC**.
- **Assign the vendor** ✅ — **② Vendor → Assign / Reassign.** This matters: an invoice can only be linked to a work order once that vendor is on the WO in CINC. The card warns you when it's missing. Status / type / vendor edits **sync to CINC** automatically.
- **Photos** ✅ — in *Photos & files*: **+ Before** / **+ After** to upload, or tag any existing photo with the **B / A** buttons. Photos are auto-compressed and mirrored to CINC. **📥 Pull from email** re-fetches the original email's photos (and runs automatically when a ticket is reclassified into a work order later).
- **Closing** ✅ — when the linked **invoice is pushed/paid**, MAIA **auto-closes the work order as paid**. For a **downpayment**, the AP team checks "Partial / downpayment" on the invoice so the WO stays open for the balance.

## B. Estimate requests (getting quotes)

1. ✅ On a work-order ticket, click **📨 Request estimates** — pick the vendors, scope, photos. MAIA emails each vendor a **secure upload link** and follows up.
2. ✅ Vendors upload their estimate via the link → it appears in the **Estimates** comparison (side-by-side, lowest flagged).
3. ✅ When you've chosen, use **Send to board**.

## C. Board members / approval

1. ✅ In **Estimates → Send to board**, MAIA shows the board members with checkboxes — the **President is pre-checked** by default. Pick who must sign.
2. ✅ Each selected member gets a **"Review & sign"** email link. The approval needs all the chosen signers.
3. ✅ When the last required signer approves, MAIA automatically:
   - builds the **official signed PDF** (the estimate + a Board Approval page with each signer's name · title · date · signature),
   - **files it on the work order** (MAIA + CINC),
   - **emails the board, you, and the winning vendor** an award notice with a download link.
4. ⏳ *Coming next:* auto-request the winner's missing **W-9 / license / COI**; a **scheduling link** so the vendor adds their crew to the **association calendar**; and notices to the losing vendors.

## D. Recurring services (contracts on a schedule)

- ✅ Each association's recurring vendors (pool, landscaping, pest, …) have a cadence; their visits show on the **association calendar**.
- ✅ **Vendor agenda** — the vendor confirms next week's **crew + day** via a link, and each crew member gets a **photo-upload link** for that visit.
- ✅ **Recurring-service complaint** — on the complaint ticket, click **🔁 Recurring-service issue**. Pick the recurring service, add **your note**, optionally the resident's email. MAIA emails the **vendor** (with the resident's photo + your note — *"fix on your next visit"*), emails you, and acknowledges the resident — instead of opening a separate work order.
- ⏳ *Coming next:* the vendor marks it **Resolved + after-photo** on their agenda visit → you **1-click confirm** (or it auto-confirms after 5 days); a repeat complaint re-opens it.

## E. Emails to vendors — quick reference

| Action | What MAIA sends |
|---|---|
| 📤 Email vendor upload link | secure link to upload estimate / invoice / job photos to a WO |
| 📨 Request estimates | quote request + automatic follow-ups |
| Board approval (on award) | award notice + signed-PDF link |
| 🔁 Recurring-service issue | "fix on next visit" + the resident's photo + your note |
